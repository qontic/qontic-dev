Q-Ontic Lab

Interactive quantum simulations and notebooks for comparing alternative representations of quantum mechanics.

Comparative Apps Pilot-Wave Apps Notebooks Research Team

Q-Ontic Lab develops browser-based tools for exploring how different quantum representations describe the same physical phenomena. The project distinguishes between standalone simulation apps and interactive notebooks: apps are designed for immediate exploration, while notebooks combine explanation, context, and embedded simulations.

Featured App: Analytical Double-Slit Simulation

A multiview simulation of the double-slit experiment comparing wavefunction evolution, collapse-style detection, pilot-wave trajectories, and branching-style descriptions.
